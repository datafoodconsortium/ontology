The Farm to Fork strategy itself includes the development of a standardized labelling system, as well as SAFE (Safe Food Advocacy Europe) has started a campaign to ask for more clarity and transparency in labels in favor of consumers. In Europe we can see a first attempt of a simpler and more immediate label to be easily accessible for the consumer, the Nutri-score, developed in France and now spread in other European countries.
